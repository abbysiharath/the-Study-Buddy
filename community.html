    Melbourne's best new restaurants you might have missed

    Do you and your friends ever want to go out for dinner after a long day of work or studying but you can’t find a great place to go. Well, here is a list of great places to go with your friends. Cambodia’s Kitchen is the only Cambodian food place in the CBD, or you can go to Paik’s noodles which is a South Korean restaurant where you can get their famous seafood noodle bowl, or you can check out Kata Kita which is an Indonesian restaurant that serves delicious slow roasted pork and ink Nasi Goreng. There are some many more restaurants to be found.Read the article to find more.

    Melbourne’s best bars behind hidden doors

    Thinking of going out for a night in Melbourne in your friends well here is a list of great bars that are only accessible by those who know where to find them. Trinket Bar is just a normal looking bar until you walk into a mysterious wardrobe that open into a hidden lounge. State of Grace is another seemingly normal place with a rooftop bar but if you are able to find the correct book in the bookshelf a hidden door opens to a stairwell that leads you to a secret bar. Heroes is famous for its “Nitro Lab Ice Cream” but it also has a secret elevator that can take you all the way up to a rooftop bar. There are many more hidden bars to be found in thearticle.

    Bricktionary: The Interactive Exhibition

    Explore Melbourne museums newest exhibit. You’ll be able to see some of the biggest and best Lego builds in Melbourne. The builds are created by the Lego brick artist Ryan McNaught. All the builds come straight from his newest book. There are even some builds form the tv show Lego Masters. There are even certain areas where you can make Lego builds. This is a great place to go with friends or family.

    Glow Golf

    Ever wanted to play Mini Golf in the dark, well now you can. All of the courses have this special paint that when it interacts with neon light it gives the appearance of glowing in the dark. You can play through 9 or 18 holes in this beautiful neon lit custom course that is themed off of different Australian Icons like the great barrier reef. It is a great place to spend time with friends although those friendships may end if they are beaten too bad. They also claim to have Australia’s smallest bar.
